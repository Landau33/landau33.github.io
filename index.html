你好，我是 Tu Yuang。 Hi, I'm Tu Yuang.

我目前在南洋理工大学机械工程硕士阶段，研究方向为机器人控制与具身智能。 当前研究主要聚焦于基于 Vision-Language-Action（VLA）模型的精细操作能力提升，围绕复杂任务中的跨场景泛化、 数据效率和实时控制稳定性展开；在方法上，我结合 Mobile ALOHA 平台进行多模态数据采集、策略微调与真实机器人评测， 系统比较不同策略在可迁移性与鲁棒性方面的表现。除当前研究外，我也有工程实践积累，包括 RoboMaster 机器人竞赛、 滚翼飞行器控制算法与仿真平台开发，以及计算机视觉定位与感知相关项目经验。 I am currently an M.Sc student in Mechanical Engineering at NTU, with research interests in robot control and embodied intelligence. My current research centers on enhancing fine-grained manipulation with Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, with a particular focus on cross-task generalization, data efficiency, and real-time control stability in complex real-world settings. Methodologically, I combine multimodal data collection on the Mobile ALOHA platform with policy fine-tuning and real-robot evaluation, and I systematically compare different policies in terms of transferability and robustness. Alongside this work, I have built solid engineering experience through RoboMaster projects, quadrotor cyclocopter algorithm and simulation development, and computer-vision-based localization and perception tasks.
